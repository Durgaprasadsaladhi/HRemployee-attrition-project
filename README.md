
# HR Employee Attrition Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes employee attrition data using Python, SQL, and Power BI to identify workforce trends,
employee turnover patterns, and key business insights.

---

## Tools & Technologies Used
- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- SQL
- Power BI

---

## Project Objectives
- Analyze employee attrition trends
- Identify high-risk employee groups
- Understand factors affecting attrition
- Build interactive HR analytics dashboard
- Generate business insights and recommendations

## Key Business Insights

- Employees aged 26–35 showed the highest attrition.
- Employees with salary below 5K had significantly higher turnover.
- Laboratory Technicians and Sales Executives experienced highest attrition.
- Research & Development department contributed most attrition cases.
- Employees working overtime were more likely to leave the organization.
- Most attrition occurred during early years at company.
- Lower job satisfaction levels were associated with higher attrition.

---

## Recommendations

- Improve employee engagement programs for younger employees.
- Reduce overtime workload to improve retention.
- Review compensation structure for lower salary bands.
- Focus retention strategies on high attrition job roles.
- Improve work-life balance initiatives.
- Increase employee satisfaction and career growth opportunities.
